Juice Shop 9: Would you like a free refill?

Björn Kimminich

You didn't think you'd get away with a German OWASP Day lacking the annual Juice Shop update? We released a new major version and it is probably our most feature-rich and beautiful release to date:

  • Facelift of UI towards Material Design standards and UX best practices
  • Extended realism during checkout with delivery addresses and payment options
  • Hacking Instructor providing tutorial scripts for newbie hackers
  • New RBAC model to distinguish admin, accountant, deluxe and normal users
  • Refactored challenge names, descriptions, hints and categories
You will also get an almost brag-free update on the projects usage stats - such as our unbelievable >3.8M Docker image pulls. All this (and probably more) gets freshly pressed into this 15min juice-talk!
